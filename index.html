Welcome to Leo’s Campground! We are a family owned business that is proud to say we have been hosting campers for over 40 years. We are located at mile marker 4.5 and are simply 1000 feet from the entrance of Key West. We are known for our outstanding staff who treats our customers as family, and our native iguanas whom are glad to be a part of the Leo’s community. Look no further for an affordable, fun, and unique place to stay on your vacation. Call us today.
